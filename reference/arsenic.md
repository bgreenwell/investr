# Concentrations of arsenic in water samples

The data give the actual and measured concentrations of arsenic present
in water samples.

## Format

A data frame with 32 rows and 2 columns.

## Source

Graybill, F. A., and Iyer, H. K. (1994) *Regression analysis: Concepts
and Applications*. Duxbury Press.

## Details

- `actual` True amount of arsenic present.

- `measured` Measured amount of arsenic present.
