# Dobson's Beetle Data

The data give the number of flour beetles killed after five hour
exposure to the insecticide carbon disulphide at eight different
concentrations.

## Format

A data frame with 8 rows and 3 columns.

## Source

Dobson, A. (2002) *An Introduction to Generalized Linear Models*.
Chapman & Hall/CRC.

## Details

- `ldose` Log dose of carbon disulphide.

- `y` Number of beetles subjected to insecticide.

- `n` Number of beetles killed.
