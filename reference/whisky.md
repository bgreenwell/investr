# Whisky data

The data give the proof (measured as twice the percentage of alcohol by
volume, denoted 2ABV) of whiskey stored in a charred oak barrel against
time in years.

## Format

A data frame with 10 rows and 2 columns.

## Source

R. Schoeneman, R. Dyer, and E. Earl. Analytical profile of straight
bourbon whiskies. Journal of the Association of Official Analytical
Chemists, 54:1247–1261, 1971.

## Details

- `age` The age of the whisky (years).

- `proof` The proof of the whisky (2ABV).
