# Bladder volume data

A series of 23 women patients attending a urodynamic clinic were
recruited for a study. After successful voiding of the bladder, sterile
water was introduced in additions of 10, 15, and then 25 ml increments
up to a final cumulative total of 175 ml. At each volume a measure of
height (H) in mm and depth (D) in mm of largest ultrasound bladder
images were taken. The product H × D was taken as a measure of liquid
volume.

## Format

A data frame with 184 rows and 3 columns.

## Source

Brown, P. (1993) *Measurement, Regression, and Calibration*. Oxford
University Press.

## Details

- `subject` The subject ID.

- `HD` The product H × D (mm^2).

- `volume` The true volume of sterile water in the bladder (ml).
